Our Story

As students ourselves, we know that everything in the journey of becoming independent is very challenging. Among those challenges, finding restaurants that are delicious, clean, and affordable is not easy. That's why we want to contribute through this website to make your academic journey a little bit easier.

Our Mission

1

Delicious - Nutritious - Affordable

The website will try to find and recommend good restaurants that are hygienic and most suitable for you

2

Connect - Share

Create a student community to share dining locations and experiences together. Build a culinary map

3

Save Time - Convenient

Save students' time when searching for restaurants, while also creating convenience and ease for students during meals

Thank you for visiting our website, we hope you find the perfect dishes for you. Don't forget to leave a comment
