Mau Dich Restaurant features a nostalgic style, reminiscent of the old days when everything was rustic, simple and full of warmth. The restaurant space is decorated in a subsidized era style: old wooden tables and chairs, time-faded yellow walls, handwritten signs and familiar items such as red thermos flasks, vintage radios, iron fans, ration stamps and milk cans used as chopstick holders.
