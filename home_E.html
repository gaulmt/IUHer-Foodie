Still haven’t found what you want?

If you have not discovered the right place yet, or the restaurant you’re looking for is missing from our reviews, don’t worry! Here are three simple steps to help you “hunt” down a spot worth trying around campus.

Observe in person

Choose bright, tidy places with clean tables, food cooked on the spot, and a neat kitchen area.

Check the crowd

Pick places that have plenty of students and steady foot traffic instead of empty seats.

Ask about prices first

Always ask clearly about the price of each dish to avoid unpleasant surprises.
